About

EyeCatcher is an international photography agency owned by the genius Armand. It had its humble beginnings in a rural area in Zamboanga City, catering to students and professionals during the late 90's. It gained traction when an environmental publishing company saw some samples of his photos of macro shots during a conference. A representative of the company contacted him of EyeCatcher's services and his as well, him being the only photographer for the said studio at that time.

This environmental publishing company was National Geographic or more commonly known today as NatGeo.

News of the studio spread like wildfire at the international scene, with Armand being the center of everything due to his natural eye for angles and details.

This made EyeCatcher the go-to photographic agency for some of the major players in the business world, including Reuters, CNN, BBC, Nike, Vogue, Good Housekeeping, Elle, Simple Lifestyle and E.T. Production Studio.

EyeCatcher has now 27 brick and mortar studios all over the globe, 7000 professional photographers, and 3,000 trainees.

Apart from this, EyeCatcher also conducts trainings and seminars at universities and colleges all over the world.
